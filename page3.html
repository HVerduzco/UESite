Magnet Programs

Dual Language Programs (DLP)

L.A. Unified offers programs in grades K-12 where students can become bilingual/biliterate, culturally competent, and compete in the global economy.

Permits with Transportation (PWT)

A Court-Ordered voluntary integration program available to students in grades 1-12 who live within a PWT sending school area.
